Green Chili Enchiladas

My husband accutually found this recipe and it has been a staple since. We usually make it with ground elk meat but beef is a great subsitute. We love this recipe because it is made with Coors Light and Bacon!

Ingrediants
Amount
Ground Beef
2 pounds
Thick Cut Bacon
5 Pieces
White Onion, chopped
1 Medium
Cumin
1 tbsp
Chipotle powder
1 tsp
Garlic powder
1 tsp
Coors Light
4 Ounces
Salt and Pepper
To Taste
Stand up Corn Tortillas
8-10
4 Cheese Mexican Style
1 cup
Las Palmas Green Chili Enchilada Sauce, Medium
28 Ounce
Green Chilies, chopped
8 ounces

    1. Over medium heat add bacon and onions to skillet. Cook until onions are translucent.
    2. Add ground meat to pan and cook until pink color is gone.
    3. Add cunin, chipotle, beer, salt and peper. Continue cooking for 5-7 minutes.
    4. Mix in half of the green sauce and all of the green chilies. Mix well.
    5. Stand tortillas up in a 9.5x11 baking pan. Fill tortillas with beef mixture.
    6. Cook in oven at 425 degrees for 15 minutes. Then top with cheese and cook for another 5 minutes until cheese is melted.
    7. Remove from oven and let cool enough to eat!
