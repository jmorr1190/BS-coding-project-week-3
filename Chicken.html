White Chicken Chili

This super simple recipe is always a hit when I don't want to cook but need something quick and easy.

Ingrediants
Amount
Chicken Breast, cubbed
1 pound
Canned Chili beans, Walmart Beans (Can be found with other beans like pinto and kidney)
1- 14 ounce can
Canned Pinto Beans
1- 14 ounce can
Cream of Chicken
1 large can
Taco seasoning (any heat type)
1 package
Cheese
Garnish
Corn tortilla chips
Garnish

    Mix all together in your crockpot. Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4.
