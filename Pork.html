Baked Beer Pork Chops

Here is another quick and easy recipe for school nights. These pork chops come out super moiste and tender. I generaly serve them over mashed potatoes with a choice of salad or veggies.

Ingrediants
Amount
Boneless thick pork chops or sliced pork loin
8
Ketchup
1 cup
BBQ sauce, your choice of heat
1 cup
Beer of your choice
12 fl oz
Brown Sugar
3/4 of a cup

    1. Mix everything together except for pork in a mixing bowl.
    2. Lay pork flat in a 9.5x11 baking dish.
    3. Poor mix over pork.
    4. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. Make sure that the pork internal temp is 145 degrees.
    5. Remove from oven and let cool before eating.
